Teaching Assistant for the undergraduate Electronics Laboratory at Northeastern University. Mentored twelve undergraduates through semester-long projects integrating digital logic, microcontrollers, and circuit design. Six students produced co-authored publications presented at the ASEE Annual Conference 2026, and six more at FYEE 2026.

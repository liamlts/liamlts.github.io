abstract: "Quantum spin liquids represent a unique class of frustrated magnetic materials on the cutting edge of research. Our collaborators have synthesized the first thin films of TbInO₃, a promising new multifunctional QSL candidate which also hosts improper ferroelectricity. We deploy advanced x-ray spectroscopy tools including XMCD and RIXS at the Tb L-edge to characterize the magnetic and electronic properties and shed light on how 4f-5d hybridization influences the magnetic exchange interactions."

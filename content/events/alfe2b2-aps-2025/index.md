abstract: "Among magnetocaloric candidate systems, AlFe₂B₂ has emerged as an earth-abundant material with a ferromagnetic transition at 280 K, ideally near ambient conditions. We present a comprehensive experimental study of single-crystal AlFe₂B₂ combining X-ray diffraction (XRD), angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (ARPES), and X-ray magnetic circular dichroism (XMCD), carried out at synchrotron facilities worldwide."

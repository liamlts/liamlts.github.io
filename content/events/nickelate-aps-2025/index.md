abstract: "The metal-to-insulator transition (MIT) in the rare-earth nickelate series, RENiO₃, remains a central open question due to the complex interplay of electron–electron interactions. We present Resonant Inelastic X-ray Scattering (RIXS) studies on nine compounds. Our results reveal that the MIT is strongly influenced by a complex crystal-field environment. We compute Wannier orbitals to constrain model parameters and perform two-site exact diagonalization calculations, enabling quantitative modeling of both XAS and RIXS spectra."

abstract: "We deploy ultrafast laser pulses to engineer the magnetic ground state of an iridium-based frustrated quantum magnet. By resonantly pumping an IR active phonon, we engineer long-lasting changes to its magnetic Hamiltonian, as demonstrated by our THz spectroscopy measurements. Our results open new pathways to controlling complex magnetic orders in strong spin-orbit coupled frustrated spin degrees of freedom."

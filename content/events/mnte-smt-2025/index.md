abstract: "The magnetic and electronic structure of altermagnetic α-MnTe exhibits large spin-splitting making it promising for spintronic applications. We present momentum and polarization-resolved resonant inelastic x-ray scattering (RIXS) measurements on α-MnTe thin films focusing on the low energy excitations. Our results provide insights into the magnetic excitation spectrum of altermagnetic α-MnTe."

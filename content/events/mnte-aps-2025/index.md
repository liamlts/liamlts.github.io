abstract: "Altermagnetic α-MnTe thin films exhibit a tunable anomalous Hall effect (AHE) controlled by epitaxial strain and field cooling. We present resonant inelastic X-ray scattering (RIXS) and X-ray magnetic circular dichroism (XMCD) measurements on MnTe films with different strain states. Our RIXS energy maps at the Mn L₃ edge reveal systematic changes in the dd excitations between samples, indicating strain-dependent modifications to the Mn local environment. Circular dichroism in the RIXS magnetic excitations is consistent with the presence of spontaneous shear strain. XMCD measurements are discussed in the context of how field cooling and strain affect DMI and spin canting."
